# Pass: Voice Scrub

## Purpose

Restore the **persona coherence** by removing the "generic assistant" residue that inevitably creeps in during drafting.

This pass is not about fooling detectors. It is about ensuring the voice remains consistent and hasn't drifted toward a neutral, helpful, or promotional "AI middle ground."

## What to Scrub

### 1. The Neutral Middle
Check if the persona’s sharp edges were sanded off. 
- **The Sign:** Use of "While X, Y is also true" when the persona should be opinionated.
- **The Fix:** Restore the persona’s stance. Pick a side.

### 2. Brochure & Performance Warmth
Remove any "enthusiasm performances" that wasn't in the immersion brief.
- **The Sign:** Evaluative adjectives (powerful, seamless, robust) or opening with "I'm excited to share..."
- **The Fix:** Delete the evaluation. Let the technical facts do the work.

### 3. Structural Parallelism
AI loves symmetrical paragraphs (Topic -> Detail -> Example -> Summary).
- **The Fix:** Break the symmetry. If a paragraph is simple, let it be two sentences. If it's a deep dive, let it be one long, dense block.

### 4. Connective Tissue (Filler)
Remove sentences that exist only to transition.
- **The Sign:** "Additionally, it is important to consider...", "Moving on to...", "In conclusion..."
- **The Fix:** Delete the sentence. Jump directly into the next point.

## Output

Return the **persona-coherent** version. If the draft already matches the immersion brief perfectly, confirm the pass and return the text.
