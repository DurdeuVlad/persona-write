# Pass: Rewrite

## Purpose

Produce the rewritten text using the persona mapping and diagnostic findings as the operating brief.

This is where the actual writing happens. Everything before this was diagnosis and planning. Everything after is cleanup and verification.

## What to do

Work from the persona mapping brief. Apply what you decided there: what to foreground, what to cut, what to assert, what to hedge.

Do not re-diagnose during the rewrite. The diagnostic audit already identified the problems. Address them.

Do not re-debate the persona. The persona mapping already decided. Apply it.

## Principles

### Preserve meaning
The meaning of the original is not up for reinterpretation. What the writer was trying to say should still be said, in the persona's voice.

When content must be cut, cut filler. Do not cut the argument.

### Do not caricature
The persona should come through in structure, compression, stance, and rhythm — not in surface quirks.

Blunt-operator does not mean rude. Sharp-technical does not mean arrogant. Clear-teacher does not mean condescending.

Every persona is a sensible, intelligent person with a clear point of view. Write them that way.

### Compress where the persona compresses
If the persona assumes reader competence, cut the explanation the reader does not need. But check that you are cutting because the reader genuinely does not need it, not because it is inconvenient to include.

### Vary sentence rhythm
A rewrite that replaces filler with tight prose but keeps identical sentence length throughout has solved one problem and created another.

The diagnostic audit should have flagged rhythm flatness. The rewrite should fix it.

### Address the diagnostic list
Go back to the diagnostic audit output. Verify each problem was addressed. It is easy to rewrite the surface of the text while leaving the structural problems intact.

## What not to do

- Do not add filler to make the rewrite feel more substantial.
- Do not soften points the persona would state directly.
- Do not add a summary sentence to every paragraph out of habit.
- Do not make the text longer than it needs to be to make the rewrite feel thorough.
- Do not restate the original structure if the persona mapping said to restructure.

## Output

The rewritten text.

If mode is **draft**, produce the draft in full.
If mode is **rewrite**, replace the original.
If mode is **refine**, apply the persona mapping to the existing text as a lighter pass.
If mode is **audit**, only produce a rewrite if the user asked for one or it is obviously useful.
